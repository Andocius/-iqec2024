About Mr. Nicodemus Shauri Eatlawe:

Mr. Nicodemus Shauri Eatlawe is an experienced Senior Education Policy Analyst, advocate for educational equity, and dedicated researcher. With over 30 years of experience in education policy analysis, development, and advocacy, he has worked to enhance educational equity and quality across Tanzania. Mr. Eatlawe holds an MA in Education and a Postgraduate Diploma in Research Methods for the Social Sciences from the University of Bradford, UK, and he intends to pursue a PhD focused on the role of NGOs in Civil Society within Tanzanian governance.

Throughout his career, Mr. Eatlawe has contributed to impactful educational initiatives in partnership with national and international organizations, including World Vision Tanzania, TEN/MET, and Maarifa ni Ufunguo. His expertise includes achievement motivation, action research, project evaluations, teacher motivation, and policy dialogue. He has been actively involved in developing and implementing education policies that address the needs of marginalized communities and advance systemic educational reform.

A passionate advocate for quality education, Mr. Eatlawe has led numerous advocacy campaigns focused on creating equitable education policies and practices. His dedication to supporting underrepresented communities is evident in his extensive work on projects that influence education sector development in Tanzania. In addition to his professional responsibilities, he participates actively in various educational forums and networks, contributing his expertise to ongoing discussions and policy developments.
