Ms. Elizabeth Mary Gupta

Chairperson, African Wild Dog Conservation Fund (AWCF) UK

About Ms. Elizabeth Mary Gupta:

Appointed as General Manager at BETL in January 2023, Ms. Elizabeth Mary Gupta spearheads the innovation of an Early Childhood Development (ECD) social enterprise in Dar es Salaam. Her work focuses on creating cost-effective, high-quality, and scalable play-based ECD services for children aged 3-5 years. In her previous role as Chief Operating Officer at Delina Oil and Gas Distributors Limited, she successfully streamlined operations and expanded the business into East Africa's dry cargo transport market.

With over 15 years of experience in education across Zambia, Zimbabwe, Nigeria, the UK, and South Africa, Ms. Gupta has demonstrated exceptional leadership as Chairperson at Charlbury Pre School and as Programme Manager at Cecily's Fund, where she supported girls' education and managed institutional funding. Her tenure at VSO in Nigeria showcased her strategic planning skills, fundraising capabilities, and partnership formation within the education sector.

Currently serving as Chairperson at the African Wild Dog Conservation Fund (AWCF) UK, Ms. Gupta leads fundraising and drives the charity’s status to support wildlife conservation, education, and community development projects in Zimbabwe.

Ms. Gupta's academic achievements include a Master’s with Distinction in Educational Planning, Economics, and International Development from UCL's Institute of Education, alongside a Bachelor's in Geography from Kings College London. Her career is marked by a deep commitment to education, community development, and a remarkable ability to advance organizational initiatives.
