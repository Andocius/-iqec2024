About Taungana Ndoro:

Taungana Ndoro is a seasoned communications professional currently serving as the Director of Communications and Advocacy at the Ministry of Primary and Secondary Education in Zimbabwe. With over 20 years of experience spanning both public and private sectors, he has proven expertise in strategic public relations, media management, and stakeholder engagement. His efforts have been pivotal in advancing new educational programs and policies within the Ministry.

Previously, Taungana held the position of Chief of Corporate Communications at the Zimbabwe Revenue Authority (ZIMRA) for 11 years, where he oversaw external messaging and managed crisis communications. His leadership roles extend beyond government service, including serving as Secretary General of the Zimbabwe Institute of Public Relations from 2017 to 2020 and acting as the Country Correspondent for Zimbabwe with the African Tax Administration Forum from 2012 to 2017.
