Ramadhan Jumanne Mwakae

Environmentalist and Climate Activist, Mazingira Plus Organisation

About Ramadhan Jumanne Mwakae:

Ramadhan J. Mwakae is a committed environmentalist and climate activist with over four years of impactful work at Mazingira Plus Organisation. His areas of expertise include biodiversity and climate change mitigation, where he focuses on innovative approaches such as organic waste diversion models.

Ramadhan's passion lies in promoting sustainable practices and inspiring communities to adopt environmentally conscious actions. Through his work, he strives to create a lasting positive impact on the environment and contribute to efforts combatting climate change.
