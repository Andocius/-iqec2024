About Hayrullo Malikov:

Hayrullo Malikov is a Social Policy expert at UNICEF Tanzania with substantial experience in social policy and public finance management. His work focuses on enhancing the capacity of government partners to create and implement national poverty reduction strategies, sectoral development programs, public finance reforms, and social protection policies.

Hayrullo has spearheaded numerous pivotal studies, including poverty analyses, public expenditure reviews, investment case assessments, and other socioeconomic evaluations. These efforts have been integral to promoting evidence-based policymaking and driving meaningful reform initiatives in various settings.
