Shaibu Mandova Athumani

National Coordinator Schools2030 Project, Aga Khan Foundation Tanzania

About Shaibu Mandova Athumani:

Shaibu Mandova Athumani currently serves as the National Coordinator for the Schools2030 Project, implemented by the Aga Khan Foundation Tanzania. With over 15 years of extensive experience in education, Shaibu has dedicated his career to teaching in public schools, managing impactful education projects, and fostering community engagement.

His work includes supporting the establishment of satellite preschools in remote villages within the Kilwa district and contributing to the whole-school approach training for the Kizazi Kipya project across Tanzania. Additionally, Shaibu played a pivotal role in implementing school- and community-centered reading programs in Lindi through the Strengthening Education Systems in East Africa project.
