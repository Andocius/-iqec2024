About Dr. Sempeho Siafu:

Dr. Sempeho Siafu is an accomplished education specialist with a focus on empowering out-of-school adolescents and marginalized communities in Tanzania. He currently serves as the Director of Academics at the Institute of Adult Education, where he leads national literacy campaigns, curriculum development, and functional literacy education. He also coordinates the Integrated Programme for Out of School Adolescents (IPOSA), impacting over 50,000 adolescents with essential education and life skills.
