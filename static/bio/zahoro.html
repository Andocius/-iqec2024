Zahoro Muhaji

Chief Executive Officer, Tanzania Startup Association

About Zahoro Muhaji:

Zahoro Muhaji is a seasoned entrepreneur and leader with over 13 years of experience in managing and scaling businesses. As the CEO of the Tanzania Startup Association (TSA) since 2019, he has been instrumental in empowering Tanzania’s startup ecosystem, advocating for policy changes, and fostering collaborations that drive growth and innovation.

In addition to his role at TSA, Zahoro serves on several national committees, including the Technical Team for the National Development Vision 2050 and the TNBC Digital Transformation Working Group, contributing to the country’s long-term strategic planning and digital transformation. He also plays a vital role in promoting financial inclusion and entrepreneurship through the National Entrepreneurship Development Committee (NEDC).

A passionate advocate for leveraging technology to address business challenges, Zahoro focuses on “open finance” initiatives to improve financial access for underserved populations. His efforts in integrating mobile money and banking data aim to make lending more inclusive and efficient.

Recognized as a thought leader in Tanzania’s business landscape, Zahoro continues to inspire and support the next generation of entrepreneurs, driving sustainable economic development across the region.
