Ms. Martha Makala

National Coordinator, TEN/MET

About Ms. Martha Makala:

Ms. Martha Makala currently serves as the National Coordinator for the Tanzania Education Network (TEN/MET), a coalition committed to promoting quality and inclusive education across Tanzania. With over a decade of experience in both local and international NGOs, Ms. Makala has played a key role in addressing policy gaps and championing gender equality in education.

Leadership at TEN/MET:

Since joining TEN/MET in May 2022, she has fostered collaboration among stakeholders to improve educational outcomes and drive policy reforms. Under her leadership, TEN/MET has launched initiatives focused on enhancing teacher welfare, integrating ICT in educational delivery, and preventing violence against children in schools. Ms. Makala’s strategic focus includes sustainable funding models, rigorous monitoring and evaluation, and transparency to ensure impactful educational interventions.

Ms. Makala remains committed to advancing TEN/MET’s mission by engaging with government bodies, development partners, and civil society organizations. As a leading figure in the education sector, her efforts to coordinate the 4th International Quality Education Conference (IQEC) demonstrate her dedication to fostering solutions for sustainable education in Africa.
