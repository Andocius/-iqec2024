Amina Saidi Nalinga

Teacher, Mnolela Primary School

About Amina Saidi Nalinga:

Amina Saidi Nalinga is a dedicated Grade 'A' teacher with 19 years of teaching experience. She specializes in teaching English, Arts, Physical Education, Geography, Environmental Studies, Science, and Social Studies across various grades. Her passion for singing infuses her teaching with creativity and enthusiasm, contributing to a positive and engaging learning environment.

As part of the Schools2030/CO-CREATE project, Amina has introduced vegetation pots as a teaching tool to promote environmental awareness among her students. This innovative approach helps connect classroom learning to real-world applications, encouraging students to form Environment Clubs and actively participate in environmental stewardship.
