Dorika Adissah Burenga

Student, Geita Secondary School

About Dorika Adissah Burenga:

Dorika Adissah Burenga is a high school student at Geita Secondary School, pursuing science subjects. She is a passionate advocate for girls' education, especially for those in underserved communities.

As one of the Kuyenda Rural Youth Champions under the Education Out Loud Project, Dorika strives to create equal educational opportunities for both boys and girls. She envisions a future where every child, regardless of gender, has the right to access quality education.
