Tobias Mulupi

Head of Partnerships, Educate! Tanzania

About Tobias Mulupi:

Tobias Mulupi is a passionate educator, development professional, and advocate for quality education across East Africa with a strong belief in the potential of Africa's youth to drive meaningful change and growth across the continent.

With a background in teaching and philosophical studies, along with over 10 years of experience in the NGO development sector, Tobias currently serves as the Head of Partnerships at Educate! Tanzania. In this role, he collaborates with national and local governments and educational stakeholders to support impactful educational reforms, innovations, and initiatives aimed at enhancing youth education, agency, and entrepreneurship skills. His focus areas include Competency-Based Education (CBE) and career readiness, emphasizing experiential learning in entrepreneurship and life skills to equip young people with essential 21st-century skills for success in today’s economy.

Tobias’s collaborative work with the Kenya Institute of Curriculum Development (KICD) and the Ministry of Education Kenya (MoE) on Community Service Learning (CSL) underscores his commitment to sustainable impact and educational reform in Competency-Based Education. Tobias also brings a dynamic perspective in empowering teachers, students, and communities through a unique co-design approach with the government of Tanzania, particularly with the Tanzania Institute of Education (TIE) and the National Examinations Council of Tanzania (NECTA). He has contributed to the revised Business studies syllabus, now compulsory across the General and TVET O’level Secondary school pathways, aimed at empowering students to become self-reliant citizens who create jobs, are employable, and can compete in national and global markets.
