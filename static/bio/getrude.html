Getrude Dyabene

Advocate of the High Court of Tanzania

LHRC Senior Program Officer for Gender, Women, Children, and Persons with Disabilities

Education: Bachelor's degree in Law and Postgraduate Diploma in Legal Practice

Getrude Dyabene is an experienced professional with expertise in public policy, gender, law, child protection, litigation, legal analysis, management, and health. She has a deep understanding of issues related to gender and gender mainstreaming, women’s rights, child protection, education, SRHR, women's health, women’s political participation, leadership, advocacy, and human rights in Tanzania.

As a dedicated feminist, Getrude tirelessly advocates for the empowerment of women in all aspects of life.
