About H.E. Ambassador Mahmoud Thabit Kombo:

H.E. Ambassador Mahmoud Thabit Kombo is the current Minister of Foreign Affairs and East African Cooperation for Tanzania. Recently, he co-chaired the Second Tanzania-Finnish Political Consultations held in Finland from October 8 to 10, 2024. This diplomatic mission aimed to strengthen bilateral relations and enhance investment opportunities in key sectors such as forestry, energy, ICT, tourism, and education.

Diplomatic Engagements:

During this visit, Ambassador Kombo was accompanied by senior officials from various Tanzanian investment and trade bodies, underscoring Tanzania's commitment to building international partnerships and fostering economic cooperation with Finland. In high-level discussions with Finland's Foreign Minister Elina Valtonen, he focused on deepening diplomatic ties and exploring avenues for mutual collaboration.
