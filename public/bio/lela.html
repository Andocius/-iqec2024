Hon. Lela Muhamed Mussa

Minister of Education and Vocational Training

About Hon. Lela Muhamed Mussa:

Hon. Lela Muhamed Mussa, born on October 11, 1982, is a Special Seat Member of Parliament representing the Chama Cha Mapinduzi (CCM) party. She began her parliamentary career in 2020 and is currently serving her first term in the House of Representatives. Hon. Lela holds a Master of Education degree from the University of Dodoma (2011) and a Bachelor of Education degree from the University of Dar es Salaam (2008).

Professional Background:

With a strong commitment to education, Hon. Lela has over a decade of academic experience. She served as an Assistant Lecturer at the State University of Zanzibar (SUZA) from 2012 to 2020 and as a Tutorial Assistant at the University of Dodoma (UDOM) from 2009 to 2012. She also taught at SOS Hermann Gmeiner Secondary School and Ole Secondary School, gaining practical experience in secondary education.

Throughout her career, Hon. Lela has been dedicated to advancing educational policies, improving access to quality education, and empowering youth through learning. Her legislative role, coupled with her deep understanding of the educational landscape, enables her to advocate for meaningful reforms that support social and educational development in Tanzania.
