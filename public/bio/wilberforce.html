Dr. Wilberforce Meena

About Dr. Wilberforce Meena:

Dr. Wilberforce Meena is an Education Technical Advisor at HakiElimu and a distinguished academic and education expert with extensive experience in the Tanzanian education sector. He holds a PhD in Educational Leadership and Management and has served in various senior positions, including as a lecturer and advisor on education policy.

Professional Background:

Dr. Meena is passionate about leveraging education as a tool for social transformation and sustainable development. His research focuses on educational reforms, school leadership, and inclusive education, contributing significantly to shaping policies that enhance quality education in Tanzania. Throughout his career, Dr. Meena has been committed to empowering educators and fostering equitable learning opportunities for all students.
