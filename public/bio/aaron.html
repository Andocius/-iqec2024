Dr. Aaron Chansa

Academic and Professional Qualifications:

  • Master’s Degree in Educational Management and Leadership
  • Bachelor’s Degree in Public Administration and Development
  • Diploma and Certificate in Teaching
  • Certificate in Computer Studies
  • Certificate in Monitoring and Evaluation (M&E)
  • Certificate in Statistical Packaging in Social Sciences (SPSS)

Awards:

  • Honorary PhD in Educational Leadership, GEPEA University of Austria

Professional Experience:

  • Taught at private and government schools in Lusaka and Copperbelt provinces
  • Leadership mentor at Zambia National Women’s Lobby
  • Speaker and panelist at national and international conferences
  • Regularly featured on national television and radio

Current Leadership Positions:

  • Board Chairperson, Green Park University College

Previous Leadership Positions:

  • Deputy Director (PR), Professional Teachers Union of Zambia (PROTUZ)
  • Board Member, Mufulira College of Education
