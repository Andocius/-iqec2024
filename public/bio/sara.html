Dr. Sara Ruto, PhD, EBS

Program Lead, Echidna Giving

About Dr. Sara Ruto:

Dr. Sara Ruto is a Program Officer at Echidna Giving, where she works to fulfill the promise of girls' education, with a particular focus on foundational learning. Her extensive career in education and research spans over 30 years, encompassing roles in teaching, academia, founding and leading non-profit organizations, and serving in government.

These diverse experiences have been pivotal in shaping her professional identity and fuel her vision for an Africa where every child, regardless of background, has the opportunity to learn to read and achieve foundational literacy. Dr. Ruto is also the Chairperson of Kisii University Council and serves on several other boards.

Dr. Ruto completed her higher education at Kenyatta University in Kenya and the University of Heidelberg in Germany.
