Nancy Sumari

Executive Director, Jenga Hub

About Nancy Sumari:

Nancy Sumari is a dedicated children’s rights advocate, the visionary Founder of The Jenga Hub, and a prominent member of the Global Future Council on Media, Entertainment, & Sport at the World Economic Forum. With over eight years of experience in integrating technology within education, she is passionate about crafting innovative learning experiences that empower children and young leaders.

Through The Jenga Hub, Nancy fosters digital creativity and design thinking, contributing to a new generation of responsible, tech-savvy learners. Nancy’s impact extends beyond education. She is an award-winning entrepreneur and a former Miss Tanzania and Miss World finalist, inspiring young Africans to dream big.

She holds a Master’s degree in Development Economics from UCL and a Bachelor’s degree in Business from the University of Dar es Salaam. Additionally, she is a published author of children’s books that celebrate Tanzanian women and educate children on their rights.

Nancy’s diverse expertise in marketing communications, new media, and creative direction positions her as a powerful advocate for quality education and children’s rights on a global scale.
