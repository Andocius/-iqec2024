Joseph Wasikhongo

National Coordinator, Elimu Yetu Coalition, Kenya

About Joseph Wasikhongo:

Joseph Wasikhongo is a seasoned advocacy and capacity-building professional with a strong focus on education and gender. He brings over 15 years of experience in the development sector, having worked at local, regional, and international levels.

Currently, Joseph serves as the National Coordinator of the Elimu Yetu Coalition, a national coalition dedicated to championing the agenda of Education for All (EFA). Under his leadership, the coalition has played an instrumental role in various significant projects aimed at improving educational outcomes and policies.

One of the key highlights of Joseph's work is providing civil society leadership during the development of the Kenya Partnership Compact process. This initiative has been supported by the Education Out Loud (E.O.L) project through the Regional Oxfam support office of Horn, East, and Southern Africa (HESA).

With a passion for fostering educational opportunities and promoting gender equity, Joseph has made significant contributions to the sector, advocating for policy changes and building capacities that benefit communities across Kenya and beyond.
