Regnard Titus Mkwese

Advocacy Officer, WeWorld Tanzania

Diploma in Information Technology

Regnard Titus Mkwese is a Communication and Advocacy Officer at WeWorld Tanzania with over six months of experience in the field. With a background in Information Technology, he has developed strong skills in creating and implementing effective communication strategies, focusing on amplifying the voices of communities, especially in education and child well-being.

Aside from his professional work, Regnard is a part-time content creator, passionate about using creativity to produce impactful content. His dedication to community engagement, empowerment, and advocacy has allowed him to make a meaningful impact through his role at WeWorld.

In his personal time, Regnard enjoys traveling, swimming, reading, exploring technology, and spending time with animals. These interests inspire his commitment to fostering growth, opportunity, and positive change in all his work and creative pursuits.
