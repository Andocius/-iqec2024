Justice Rutenge

Executive Director, Foundation for Civil Society, Tanzania

About Justice Rutenge:

Justice Rutenge has been serving as the Executive Director at the Foundation for Civil Society (FCS) since May 2024. In this role, he leads the organization's mission to foster a vibrant civil society by providing grants and implementing capacity-strengthening programs. Justice brings over 14 years of experience in the development sector, with expertise in results measurement, program and strategy design, strategic communication, and resource mobilization.

Before joining FCS, Justice founded and managed iDev Tanzania Limited and iDev Consulting, where he offered advisory services to national NGOs, donor agencies, multilateral organizations, global philanthropies, and corporate clients. He holds a BA in Integrated Community Development from Daystar University and an MSc in Media, Communication, and Development from the London School of Economics and Political Science (LSE). He is currently pursuing a PhD in Business and Management at Strathmore University Business School.

Justice is an alumnus of the Mandela Washington Fellowship (MWF) and the Program for African Leadership (PfAL) at LSE. He is also an avid writer with over 50 published op-eds addressing various national, regional, and global issues.
