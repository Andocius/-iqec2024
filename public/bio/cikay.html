About Cikay Richards:

Cikay Richards is a distinguished leader recognized for inspiring change and making a significant impact, driven by a passion for growth and female leadership. She is well-known across the business landscapes of Africa, Europe, and Latin America for her strategic acumen, commitment to public service, and ability to foster strategic partnerships and collaboration.

Ms. Richards has an outstanding track record in securing multi-million-dollar contracts and managing large-scale projects, exemplified through her role as Founder and Partner of Rehoboth Advisory Limited. As the CEO of Lyra in Africa Tanzania, she showcased her strategic vision and commitment to sustainable development. During her tenure at EY Switzerland as Africa Lead for the International Development team, she demonstrated exceptional results under tight deadlines, driving profit growth, cost reduction, and process enhancement. Her decisive leadership and communication skills have earned widespread respect and admiration.

Ms. Richards holds an M.Phil. in International Peace Studies from Trinity College Dublin, a BA Honors in International Relations from Dublin City University, an Advanced Diploma in Project Management, and a Professional Certification in Project Management from the Institute of Project Management in Ireland.

In 2021, she was recognized as one of the top 50 Women in Management Africa, exemplifying her dedication to public service and female empowerment. Fluent in several languages, she embodies the values of a global citizen, bridging cultures and promoting impactful change.

Cikay Richards serves on the boards of three esteemed organizations and is a respected advisor and mentor, demonstrating her visionary leadership and commitment to fostering a fair and equitable world.
