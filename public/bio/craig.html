Craig Ferla

Country Director (Tanzania), Children in the Crossfire

About Craig Ferla:

Craig Ferla is the Country Director for Children in the Crossfire in Tanzania, an INGO with a focus on early childhood development (ECD). With 25 years of leadership experience in country and regional director roles within CSOs across Tanzania, Zambia, and the wider region, Craig's programming expertise spans health and education, and more recently, ECD.

Since 2016, Craig has spearheaded initiatives that demonstrate good practices in scaling up quality pre-primary education. He has also been pivotal in pioneering support for childcare and ECD within high-density urban day care sectors. Under his leadership, Children in the Crossfire has championed policy processes and system strengthening, highlighted by the launch and ongoing implementation of Tanzania’s first National Multisectoral Early Childhood Development Programme 2021/22 – 2025/26 (NM-ECDP).

As a local policy expert, Craig has played a significant role in navigating and influencing civil society policy in collaboration with national and local government. He has contributed to pre-primary education and ECD dialogues, including the roadmap for developing and implementing the NM-ECDP. Fluent in Swahili, Craig has built extensive networks within Tanzania, having lived there predominantly since 1993. He holds a degree in Geography and Swahili and an MSc in International Development.
