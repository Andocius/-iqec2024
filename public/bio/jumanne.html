Jumanne Mtambalike

Chief Executive Director, Sahara Ventures

About Jumanne Mtambalike:

Jumanne Rajabu Mtambalike is a Tanzanian innovator, technology enthusiast, entrepreneur, social reformer, blogger, and author. He is best known for his dedication to building stable innovation ecosystems in Africa. Jumanne co-founded and currently serves as the Chief Executive Officer of Sahara Ventures, a company established in 2015 to foster innovation, technology, and entrepreneurship in Africa. Sahara Ventures encompasses Sahara Consulting, Sahara Accelerator, and Sahara Sparks.

Jumanne's career milestones include being the first manager of Buni Innovation Hub and spearheading "Silicon Dar," Tanzania’s first technology district in Dar es Salaam. In both 2017 and 2018, he was recognized as the most influential Tanzanian in science and technology. He is a renowned speaker advocating for the use of innovation and technology to address Africa’s most pressing challenges and has contributed to platforms such as Smart Monkey TV and VC4Africa.

Mtambalike is a strong proponent of women's participation in ICT and has initiated programs like She-Codes for Change, She-Found Africa Initiative, and Buni Divaz. He developed the first training manual for She-Codes for Change, teaching young girls coding and application development. He has supported numerous innovation hubs and business incubators in East Africa and mentored at Ndoto Hub, a female-led Tanzanian business incubator. Through AfriLabs, he conducts training for the sustainability of African tech hubs.

Jumanne is also involved in youth empowerment through initiatives like the Tanzania AI Lab and has served as a senior consultant for COSTECH. His educational background includes a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and a Master’s degree in Information Technology and Management, followed by a postgraduate diploma in ICT leadership from Dublin City University.
