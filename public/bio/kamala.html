Kamala Dickson

Former Chair – SADC Youth Parliament Committee

About Kamala Dickson:

Kamala Dickson is a young leader from Tanzania committed to promoting global peace and sustainability. He currently serves as a member of the UNOCT’s Working Group on Youth, Sports, and Preventing Violent Extremism and is the Head of the Pan-Africanism, Sports, Arts, Culture & Heritage Cluster. Kamala is a former Chair of the SADC Youth Parliament Committee, a member of Generation Democracy, a Climate Justice Activist, Global Youth Ambassador, Peace Ambassador, Sustainable Development Goal Ambassador, and an active, concerned citizen.

He has been recognized as a Young Leader Initiative award recipient and participated in the United Nations Union Sports Council Youth Forum in Accra, Ghana, through the UNOCT’s Global Sports Programme. Kamala is also an alumnus of the Africa Center for Strategic Studies.

Currently working with Agenda Participation Initiative (API), a Tanzanian NGO advocating for democratic governance and accountable service delivery, Kamala has held leadership roles in numerous youth-led regional and international organizations. As a former East African Community Youth Ambassador (EAC-YAP), he has facilitated and engaged in various youth-led initiatives at the East African Community and the African Union. Kamala is an advocate for the establishment of a national youth council in Tanzania and a regional East African Community youth council, emphasizing youth involvement in economic and political matters and gender inclusion.

An experienced campaigner, Kamala has run for the East African Legislative Assembly and participated in various national elections as an observer. He leverages social media to inspire and empower young people, training them to use technology for income generation and advocacy. Kamala’s online presence, with over 35,000 followers on Twitter, allows him to engage in campaigns on peace, leadership, and climate change, reaching a wide audience and amplifying youth voices.

As a Global Youth Ambassador, Kamala champions the right to inclusive education for all children, including those in remote areas. He believes in the power of youth as drivers of national development and is an alumnus of the Young Leaders Forum (YLF) under FES Tanzania. He has represented Tanzanian youth at events such as the 2018 Global Peace Leadership Conference in Uganda and various other youth-led events.

Kamala’s motto: “Organize, don't agonize”
