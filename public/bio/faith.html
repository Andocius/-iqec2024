Faith Mbushi

Strategy and Growth Director, Dignitas Project

About Faith Mbushi:

Faith Mbushi is a dedicated professional with 15 years of experience in the development sector, focusing on girls' education programs. As the Strategy and Growth Director at Dignitas Project in Kenya, she has demonstrated a deep commitment to equity and inclusion.

Her expertise spans evidence generation, impact evaluations, and policy advocacy. Faith holds a Master’s Degree in Social and Public Policy from the University of York and is known for her work as a skilled qualitative researcher who employs mixed-methods to tackle complex social challenges.

Faith excels in collaborative settings, working with researchers, program implementers, and policymakers to translate research findings into actionable insights. As part of the Education Evidence for Action (EE4A) team, she has developed strong relationships with key stakeholders, including those in the Ministry of Education.

Her current focus is on maximizing impact through evidence-based, inclusive, and sustainable strategies. Faith’s compassionate leadership style empowers others to reach their full potential and devise solutions to complex problems. She believes deeply in adding value and contributing holistically to her team and the larger community.

Faith is inspired by the quote from Albert Einstein, “When you stop learning, you start dying.” She upholds the belief that learning is continuous and extends far beyond formal education. As someone shaping future generations, she values lifelong learning and recognizes its profound power to transform lives.
