Dr. Ador Riak Nyiel

National Coordinator | Education Coalition, South Sudan

About Dr. Ador Riak Nyiel:

Dr. Ador Riak Nyiel, PhD, is a distinguished academic and a passionate advocate for the right to education for all. He holds a Doctorate Degree in Education (Education in Emergencies) from the University of Nairobi, Kenya, and additional degrees including a master’s in Education in Emergency, a Master of Arts in Humanitarian and Conflict Studies, and a bachelor’s in Education (English and Literature).

With over 15 years of expertise, Dr. Nyiel has contributed extensively to policies related to Education in Emergencies, girls’ education, teacher education, education management, youth engagement, and coalition building among Civil Society Organizations (CSOs). He started his teaching career in 2004 and shifted to humanitarian work in 2014, eventually becoming the National Coordinator for the Partners on Education Group, a civil society network advocating for educational rights.

In 2016, Dr. Nyiel co-founded the National Education Coalition (NEC), a structured network of over 180 national and international CSOs dedicated to educational advocacy. He currently serves as the National Coordinator of the NEC, which is hosted by Oxfam South Sudan. Dr. Nyiel is also an Assistant Professor of Education at Upper Nile University and actively participates in numerous educational initiatives, including the Local Education Group and various Technical Working Groups under the Ministry of General Education and Instruction.
