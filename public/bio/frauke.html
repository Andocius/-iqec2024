Frauke Heinze

Regional Director, DVV International

About Frauke Heinze:

Frauke Heinze is an international public health and adult education expert with more than 25 years of experience in international cooperation across Africa and Asia. She currently serves as the Regional Director for DVV International in East and the Horn of Africa. With a strong passion for enhancing national systems to ensure quality health and non-formal youth and adult education services, Frauke actively contributes to the development of effective policies, implementation frameworks, and educational curricula.

In her role, she works closely on initiatives aimed at research and development of community-oriented education programs, vocational skills training, and behavior change interventions. Her work reflects her commitment to addressing the educational and health needs of communities by creating sustainable solutions through policy and practice. Frauke's approach focuses on empowering communities and building local capacities to achieve long-term impact.
