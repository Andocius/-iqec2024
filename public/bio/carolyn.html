Carolyn Kandusi

Senior Program Officer, Segal Family Foundation

About Carolyn Kandusi:

Carolyn Kandusi is a social entrepreneur, impact investor, and policy innovator with extensive expertise in international development, civil society movement building, and government advisory work. She holds an MSc in Governance and Leadership and is dedicated to empowering visionary leaders and fostering partnerships that drive African development.

As a Senior Program Officer at the Segal Family Foundation, Carolyn has directed over $10 million in impact investments to support civil society organizations (CSOs) and social enterprises in East Africa. She is an alumnus of the European University Institute Policy Leader Fellow (2023-24), where she focused on advancing policy solutions for social change.

Carolyn leads the Policy Innovation Lab Africa, a Pan-African initiative that promotes policy innovation by creating a robust partnership framework between CSOs, social enterprises, and the public sector. This initiative finances social-led innovations and scales them through government infrastructure using evidence-based pilot projects and programs.

Passionate about strategic partnerships, Carolyn is committed to directing resources where they are most needed and enhancing the potential of social innovators. She serves on the National Advisory Committee for President Samia Suluhu Hassan and contributes to the Generation Equality Forum hosted by UN Women, where she advocates for economic justice and human rights for all.
