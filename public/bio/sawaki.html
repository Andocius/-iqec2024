Anna Sawaki

Director of Program and Partnership, CAMFED Tanzania

About Ms. Anna Sawaki:

Ms. Anna Sawaki is the Director of Program and Partnership at CAMFED Tanzania, where she plays a critical role in implementing and scaling initiatives aimed at supporting education for marginalized girls and empowering young women. Focused on transforming educational opportunities, Anna leads programs that enable young women to achieve financial independence through skills training and entrepreneurship.

Impact and Key Initiatives:

Anna has been instrumental in launching CAMFED’s innovative programs, including the Learner Guide Program, which trains young women to mentor students and support their communities. Her work also extends to fostering partnerships with government and non-governmental stakeholders to secure sustainable impacts in education, especially for vulnerable girls in rural areas.

Under Anna’s leadership, CAMFED has strengthened collaborative efforts to remove barriers to education and promote gender equality, aligning with Tanzania's national development goals. Her dedication to education and women’s empowerment continues to drive CAMFED’s mission to bring systemic change to the lives of young women across Tanzania.
