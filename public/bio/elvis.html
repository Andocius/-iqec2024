Elvis Chitsungo

Executive Director, Education Coalition of Zimbabwe (ECOZI)

About Elvis Chitsungo:

Elvis Chitsungo is the Director of the Education Coalition of Zimbabwe (ECOZI). He is a passionate, seasoned, and innovative educationist with over 20 years of experience in the field. Elvis is committed to transforming education practices to enhance students' learning experiences and create inclusive, learner-centered environments that promote holistic development.

Driven by his desire to influence education policy and practice, Elvis has been a key figure in driving positive changes in education and inspiring educators to explore new approaches that nurture the potential of every student.

Elvis is also known for his philanthropic efforts, actively supporting initiatives that address education, poverty alleviation, and healthcare in Zimbabwe. He uses his platform to raise awareness about various social and environmental issues. His contributions have earned him numerous awards and accolades, highlighting his dedication to improving lives through education and community service.
